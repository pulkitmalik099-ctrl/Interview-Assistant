==================================================
              COPILOT CONTEXT DIRECTORY           
==================================================

Drop your resume, cover letter, prep notes, or specific 
interview questions/cheat-sheets into this directory.

Supported Formats:
- Text Files (*.txt)
- PDF Files (*.pdf)

How it works:
On startup, the Copilot will automatically read all text and PDF files in this folder, extract their content, and provide it as context to the Gemini model. This allows Gemini to answer questions using your specific experience, projects, and skills.

Example:
If your resume states you have 3 years of experience with React and Django, and the interviewer asks "What is your tech stack?", the Copilot will automatically formulate a response outlining React and Django.
